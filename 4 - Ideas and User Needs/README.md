# Ideas and User Needs

## Overview
This module explores the critical first phase of product management: identifying where great ideas come from and how to separate "noisy" requests from the real underlying needs of users and customers.

## Core Concepts
### 1. The Origin of Ideas
*   Where ideas come from as a PM (internal stakeholders, customers, market trends).
*   How to build a system for collecting and prioritizing these ideas.

### 2. Getting to Real User Needs
*   Techniques for digging beneath the surface of what a user *says* they want to find what they actually *need*.
*   Separating the "Signal" (valuable insight) from the "Noise" (distraction).

### 3. Users vs. Customers
*   Understanding the fundamental difference between the person who *uses* the product and the person who *pays* for it.
*   Why this distinction is critical for business-to-business (B2B) products.
